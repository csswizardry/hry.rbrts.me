Hi there! I am Harry Roberts. I am a 23 year old designer, developer, writer, speaker and front-end architect from the UK. I currently work as Senior UI Developer for BSkyB. I Tweet at @csswizardry.

I specialise in authoring and scaling massive front-ends. I write on the subjects of maintainability, architecture, performance, OOCSS and more at csswizardry.com. I am the lead and sole developer of inuit.css, a powerful, scalable, Sass-based, BEM, OOCSS framework.

Speaking

I speak at international conferences on the subjects of front-end development and architecture. To see a list of past and upcoming speaking events, or to book me to speak at a conference of your own, please see my speaking page.
